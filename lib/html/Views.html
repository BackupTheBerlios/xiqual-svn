Views

view_new
Allocate a small chunk of display memory with back store.
view_free
Deallocate an xiview and its surface(s).
view_show
Blit view image to surface it is created for.
view_hide
Restore the area behind a view from its backing store.
Views are rectangular screen-areas with backing-store. This is more or less a general-purpose "windowing" system for SDL-based program, or the foundation of one. It can be used for the mouse pointer, sprites, requesters, message boxes and heaps of other things.
